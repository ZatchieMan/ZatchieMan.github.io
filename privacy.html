Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Iron Log ("the app") is a strength-training tracker. This page explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and how you can control it. Iron Log is developed and operated by an individual developer, not a company.

Information we collect

If you use the app without signing in ("Use offline on this device"), your data stays on your device only, and none of the below is collected by us.

If you sign in with Google, we collect and store:

  • Account info: your email address, name, and profile picture, as provided by Google Sign-In.
  • Workout data you enter: exercises, sets, reps, weights, training programs, personal records, and bodyweight/measurement logs.
  • App preferences: unit settings (kg/lb, cm/ft-in) and reminder preferences.
  • Push notification data: if you enable workout or weight-log reminders, we store a device push subscription token so we can deliver that notification. We don't collect this unless you turn a reminder on.
  • Friend/social data: if you use the friends feature, we store friend connections and the email you used to send a request, so the other person can find and accept it.

How we use this information

Solely to provide the app's functionality: syncing your workout data across your devices, showing your training history and progress, and sending the reminders you explicitly enable. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Where your data is stored

Your account data is stored using Supabase (a hosted database and authentication provider). The app itself is hosted on Netlify. Sign-in is handled by Google. Each of these providers processes data on our behalf under their own security and privacy practices.

Your controls

  • Export: Profile → Settings → "Export CSV" downloads your full workout history.
  • Delete your data: Profile → Settings → "Reset all data" permanently erases your exercises, workouts, and programs from our servers and your device.
  • Delete your account entirely: email us at the address below and we'll remove your account and all associated data.
  • Turn off reminders: Profile → Settings, toggle either reminder off at any time — this also removes the associated push subscription.

Children's privacy

Iron Log is not directed at, and should not be used by, children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
